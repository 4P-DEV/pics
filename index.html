About 4P: SFU's Next Generation of Innovators

4P is a dynamic group of four passionate and skilled computing science students from Simon Fraser University in Burnaby, BC. Comprised of Pratham Garg, Krish Bedi, Gourav Sharma, and our team leader, Ruben Dua, we are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of software engineering and development. Our collective expertise spans various areas of computer science, and we are actively seeking opportunities to apply our skills to real-world projects. This gallery showcases our individual talents and our collaborative spirit.
